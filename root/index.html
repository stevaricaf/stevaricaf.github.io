Who am i & what i do?

I am computer programmer who specialize in website design and development. My duties include determining the structure and design of your web pages, striking a balance between functional and aesthetic design, and ensuring web design is optimized for touch devices.

I specialize in making things for my clients. Most of the things I make are digital interfaces, but I also put effort into web design.

I'll fulfill your ideas and suggestions, combine it with my expertise and create you a 'User-Friendly', 'Easy-to-Use', clean and memorable website that will leave a powerful impression on users.
